Hiking

This year, 2017, was actually the first time I have ever gone hiking. It has always been an interest of mine since I am from a small farm town in Illinois. By me, all you see is corn and cows licking windows in the library. Hiking allowed me to see the world from a different level (no pun intended). It is definitely tough to get up the mountains but it is well worth it -- just look at the views. They are breathtaking.

Although I have only climbed a few mountains, I plan on continuing throughout my years here in college and as long as I possibly can.
